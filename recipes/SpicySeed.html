Spicy Seed Mix

For a healthy snack nibble on this mix of sunflower, pumpkin and linseeds. You might also like to sprinkle on salads and soup.

Ingredients

  • 250 grammes of mixed seed (sunflower, pumpkin, linseed)
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ras-el-hanout
  • l/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of agave syrup

Steps

Step 1

Preheat oven to 160 C.

Step 2

Put all the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

Step 3

Spread ingredients evenly on a baking sheet.

Step 4

Place baking sheet with ingredients into oven for 20 minutes. Stir once at about 10 minutes in.

Step 5

Allow to cool and store in a sealed container for up to 3 weeks.
